{\large The goal of our project is to create a simple application for ordering and posting Traditional food.The realization of the project started assembling data about what people need the most during COVID 19 pandemic Which is food , so we decided to create an application for ordering and posting . For this purpose , we collected information about variety of similar systems to avoid their mistakes and benefit from what the people who worked on it did right . Then , we worked on this notes to develop our app and tried to make sure that we make as close as possible to our vision and expectations and make this app special and different from the others  . Finally , we developed an app that is easy and simple to use , an app that achieves our vision .  }
