\subsection{Problematic and Contribution}
\hspace{0.5 cm} The traditional method of ordering food faces many problems such as wasting a lot of human energy and material resources,
The food delivery sector has become at the forefront of the problem sectors of the demand-driven economy after it recorded remarkable growth thanks to the use of modern technologies that allow customers to register their orders more quickly and easily and at the lowest cost with the click of a button
and at the present time especially in light of the Corona crisis that affected all over the world, where more than 1.5 billion people have been forced to stay in their homes around the world, due to the quarantine decisions imposed by the authorities, in order to curb the spread of the new Corona virus, and this new reality has brought about a great demand for delivery services.

To solve this problem, we are going to develop a system, which is designed mainly aim to help people to have a user-friendly Online Food Ordering  application.
